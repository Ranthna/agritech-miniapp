Common Questions

How long should I dry my rice? ‚ñº
Rice should be dried until moisture content reaches 14% or lower. This typically takes 2-3 days in good sun conditions. Use a moisture meter for accurate measurements.
What temperature is best for crop storage? ‚ñº
Most crops should be stored at 10-15¬∞C with humidity below 60% to prevent spoilage. Ensure proper ventilation in storage areas.
How do I prevent pest infestation? ‚ñº
Ensure proper cleaning of storage areas, use sealed containers, and maintain low moisture levels. Regular inspection is crucial. Consider using natural pest deterrents.
When is the best time to apply fertilizer? ‚ñº
Apply fertilizer during early morning or late evening to minimize evaporation. For rice, split applications work best: at planting, tillering, and flowering stages.
